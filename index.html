  2. 2.
    IoT-Based Automated Water Conservation & Plant Irrigation System

    Mentor Names: Dr. Parul Tomar and Dr. Sonam Khera

    Skills Used: IoT, Embedded Systems, Automation, Sensor Integration

    Timeline: 28/10/2024 - 12/12/2024

    This project automates the repurposing of Reverse Osmosis (RO) wastewater for sustainable plant irrigation. The system collects wastewater in a storage container monitored by a water level sensor to prevent overflow. A soil moisture sensor tracks soil humidity, and when levels drop below the threshold, a relay module activates a water pump to irrigate the plants. A buzzer alerts when the container is full, ensuring timely intervention. This eco-conscious solution minimizes water wastage and reduces reliance on freshwater resources.
